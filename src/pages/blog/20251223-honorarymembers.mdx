Hello HomeworkSMP!
Merry Christmas and Happy Holidays to you all! It is almost the end of the year, which means it's time to do something special!

This year, I wanted to introduce something new to thank you guys for sticking around. Hopefully this is something I will do annually from now on.
Introducing the year-end awards, awarded to players who made the most impact on the server this year.

Without further ado:

## Community Pillar Award

This Award is given to the member who is consistently active, helpful, and respected.
Congratulations **william7709**! Your efforts in helping out the community, resolving conflicts, and leading massive infrastructure projects earned you this award.

## Builder of the Year

This Award is given to the member who had the best and most ambitious construction projects of the year.
Congratulations **GrandmasterMud**! Your projects on Far Isle near the end of the year were very impressive. Keep up the projects!

## Most Impactful Player

This Award is given to the member who shaped the server the most this year.
Congratulations **BlueCreeper512**! Your involvement in the server helped the community stay active and grow. We appreciate your involvement.

## Newcomer of the Year

This Award is given to a member who joined this year and made themselves matter.
Congratulations **ridgewayplus**! Your efforts to convert the original spawn into a livable community (Chicago) did not go unnoticed!

## Server MVP

This Award is given to the member that stoodout the most to you! Yes, you! You get to vote who gets this award! Vote now at https://forms.gle/y4tk8TnkaxeKm8jG6 or DM Acto to vote. Voting will close on Wednesday, December 31st, 2025 00:00 UTC. Winner of the Server MVP will be announced Wednesday, December 31st, 2025 12:00 UTC.

Didn't get an award this year? Don't worry, keep playing and you efforts will not go unnoticed!
Thank you all once again for sticking around, and have a great 2025, hope to continue the stories of HomeworkSMP with you in 2026!
